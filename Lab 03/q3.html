Choosing the Right Laptop Without Losing Your Mind

Buying a laptop used to be simple. Now? Not so much. Between processors, RAM, SSDs, and display types, it’s easy to feel overwhelmed. But here’s the trick: focus on what you actually do. If you’re into gaming, prioritize GPU and cooling. If you’re a student or coder, go for battery life and keyboard comfort. Your laptop should fit your life—not the other way around.
